## Required attributes

### `ancestors` {#ancestors}

**Description:** Parents in the thread.\
**Type:** Array of [Status](status.md)\
**Version history:** Added in 0.6.0

### `descendants` {#descendants}

**Description:** Children in the thread.\
**Type:** Array of [Status](status.md)\
**Version history:** Added in 0.6.0
